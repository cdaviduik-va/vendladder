Resources

Here are some resources to help you get started with Starcraft 2! Even though some of them might be a bit out of date the basics have not changed much so there is still a lot to be gained. If you have any questions contact Chris Daviduik. I would also be happy to go over the game with you if you need help getting started...or at all.
  • The Reddit Wiki has a lot of information for everyone whether you are new to the game or a seasoned veteran.
  • One of my personal favourites is FilterSC's bronze to master series. In particular there is one for Terran that I would recommend if you are new to the game since most of the skills will transfer to the other races you play.
  • Blizzard has a site dedicated to each Unit in Starcraft 2 with in depth information including strengths and weaknesses against other units. This is great if you can't seem to beat a specific composition and need to learn what counters it.
  • If you are interested in watching professionals play Starcraft 2, whether you do or not, you cannot go wrong with The GSL. Aside from the highest quality games it also boasts some of the best commentating you can ask for.
  • For all other information your best bet is to check Blizzard's WCS website which includes information on current tournaments and player rankings as well as news about Starcraft 2 in general.
