Rules & Etiquette

Welcome to the VendAsta Starcraft 2 Ladder! Please follow these guidelines to ensure everyone has the best experience possible. If you have any questions or concerns please contact Chris Daviduik.
  1. You are expected to play your match within two weeks after it is assigned.

    Failure to play your match in time will result in the match being closed and each participant receiving 1 strike.

  2. You are expected to show up to your games on time. This means ensuring your Starcraft 2 application has been recently updated.

    Delaying a game will result in each person on the offending team receiving 1 strike.

  3. If a player receives 3 strikes they will be reprimanded accordingly.

    In most cases this will result in a temporary ban from participating in the VendAsta Starcraft 2 Ladder.
